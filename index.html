About Cagayan Valley Anglers Club

Who We Are

Founded in 2015, the Cagayan Valley Anglers Club is a passionate community of saltwater fishing enthusiasts dedicated to promoting sustainable fishing practices in Northern Cagayan, particularly in the municipalities of Gonzaga and Santa Ana.

Prime Fishing Spots in Cagayan Valley

Top Fishing Locations in Gonzaga

Gonzaga offers diverse fishing opportunities from shore, boat, and kayak targeting species like GT, trevally, tuna, and more.

San Jose Point

Best for: Giant Trevally, Spanish Mackerel

Rocky point with strong currents. Accessible by boat. Prime spot at high tide.

Magapit Reef

Best for: Coral Trout, Snapper

Shallow reef system known for great light tackle fishing. Watch for coral heads.

Baua River Mouth

Best for: Barramundi, Mangrove Jack

Estuary fishing at its finest. Best fished on incoming tide with live bait.

Calayan Beach

Best for: Surf Perch, Bonefish

Long sandy beach perfect for surf fishing. Great for beginners and experienced anglers alike.

Pattao Point

Best for: Yellowtail Kingfish, Tuna

Deep water point with strong currents. Excellent for trolling and jigging.

Minanga Cove

Best for: Grouper, Snapper

Protected cove with rocky bottom. Great for bottom fishing and kayak fishing.

Isca Reef

Best for: Coral Trout, Emperor

Shallow reef system with clear waters. Perfect for light tackle and fly fishing.

Amunitan Point

Best for: Wahoo, Spanish Mackerel

Deep water point with strong currents. Excellent for trolling and jigging.

Tabbac Beach

Island Fishing Adventures in Santa Ana

Santa Ana is famous for its island fishing opportunities with Palaui Island being a premier destination for both sport fishing and scenic beauty.

Palaui Island

Best for: Dogtooth Tuna, Wahoo

World-class game fishing around this protected island. Expect strong currents.

Apulagan Island

Best for: Coral Trout, Emperor

Healthy reef system with excellent bottom fishing. Protected from most winds.

Board of Directors

Meet the passionate individuals who guide our club's mission and activities.

Glenn Salvador

President

A respected leader in the local fishing community with decades of hands-on angling experience. Committed to preserving marine biodiversity and promoting the cultural and recreational importance of saltwater fishing, he inspires conservation and supports sustainable practices among anglers and coastal residents.

Vin Baloloy

Vice President

A passionate community leader and lifelong angler committed to promoting responsible fishing, organizing local tournaments, and building strong connections with fisherfolk. He works to protect marine life, support sustainable practices, and bring the angling community closer through events and education.

Mark Angelo Doctolero

Secretary

A tech-savvy student and avid angler who built and manages our website. As Secretary, he handles communications, keeps meeting records, and updates the site with news, catch reports, and events. His work helps bring new anglers into the community and keeps everything running smoothly online.

Ronel Villarao Ugale

Treasurer

A seasoned giant trevally angler hailed as a 'GT god hunter' across Gonzaga and its coastal waters. With a sharp eye for both big game and budget details, he ensures financial integrity while fueling the passion that drives our events, sponsorships, and conservation efforts.
